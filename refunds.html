Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Refund Policy outlines the terms under which Nimbus ("we", "us", or "our") issues refunds for paid subscriptions to our Service.

1. Free Trial

Nimbus offers a free Starter plan with no credit card required. We encourage you to use the free plan to evaluate the Service before upgrading to a paid subscription.

2. Subscription Refunds

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for new paid subscriptions. If you are not satisfied with the Service within the first 7 days of your paid subscription, contact us at support@usenimbus.ai and we will issue a full refund.

3. After the 7-Day Period

After the initial 7-day period, subscriptions are non-refundable. You may cancel your subscription at any time and you will continue to have access to the paid features until the end of your current billing period.

4. Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account billing settings or by contacting us. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. We do not provide partial refunds for unused time within a billing period.

5. Exceptions

We may issue refunds outside of this policy at our sole discretion in cases of:

  • Extended service outages caused by us
  • Duplicate charges or billing errors
  • Other exceptional circumstances reviewed on a case-by-case basis

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at support@usenimbus.ai with your account email and reason for the refund. We will respond within 2 business days.

7. Contact

For any billing or refund questions, contact us at support@usenimbus.ai.
